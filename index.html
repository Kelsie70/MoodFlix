Moodflix



enter movie title



Moodflix will find a review for your movie and determine the different emotions(joy, sadness, anger, disgust, fear) towards the movie.




About Us

We are the Jay Fan Club, comprised of Asheton Harrell, Phillip Griggs, Kelsie Belan, and John Little. We had a vision of using textual analysis on a plethora of reviews from various sources in order to determine the overall feeling towards the movie being searched. This can help with better categorizing genres of movies, give more metadata for a movie searching algorithm, and much more. While we could not grab multiple reviews, as there were no APIs that could complete this task, here is a prototype of the idea!
